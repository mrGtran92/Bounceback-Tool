General Medicine

Does this bounceback belong to the long call team?

After 4 PM, long call can admit their own bouncebacks only if they have not capped — 20 total patients, or the bounceback intern already has 10.

Routing — ED weekend

General Medicine

Does this bounceback belong to the long call team or a non-call team?

On weekends, only one GMed team is on long call. All other GMed teams are non-call.

Routing — ED weekend, long call

General Medicine

What time is it now?

Long call can admit their own bouncebacks up to 6 PM on weekends.

Routing — ED weekend, non-call team

General Medicine

What time is it now?

Non-call teams can accept bouncebacks before noon. Between noon and 4 PM, long call admits and holds over.

Routing — ED weekend

Geriatrics

What time is it now?

Geri uses the same weekend pipeline as GMed non-call teams. Before noon the Geri team admits directly; noon–4 PM GMed long call admits and holds over; after 4 PM NAR admits.

Routing — ED weekend, Geri noon–4 PM

Geriatrics

Is GMed long call capped?

Between noon and 4 PM, GMed long call admits the Geri bounceback and holds over — as long as long call has not capped (20 total patients, or the intern has 10).

Routing — ED

Cardiology

What time is it now?

The on-call Cardiology team admits from 7 AM to 4 PM. The Cardiology night admitting resident takes over after 4 PM.

Routing — ICU weekday

The ICU must notify the accepting team by 4 PM for the transfer to proceed today.

Routing — ICU weekend

General Medicine

Does this bounceback belong to the long call team or a non-call team?

Long call has no noon cutoff for ICU bouncebacks on weekends. Non-call teams must be notified by noon.

Non-call teams on weekends must be notified by noon for the transfer to proceed today.
