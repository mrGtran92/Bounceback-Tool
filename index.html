Step 1 of 5 — Eligibility

Was this patient cared for by this team within the past 14 days?

Counts from the date the team last had primary responsibility. Night float and cross-cover do not count.

Step 2 of 5 — Eligibility

Is the chief concern cardiac, or a problem actively addressed during the prior admission?

Cardiology bouncebacks require the chief concern to be cardiac in nature or a previously managed issue. A new unrelated problem does not qualify.

Step 3 of 5 — Eligibility

Is today the original intern's last day of service?

If yes, the patient cannot bounce back regardless of any other circumstances. This rule is absolute.

Step 4 of 5 — Eligibility

Is the original intern present today?

If the intern is off, the answer depends on whether the current resident previously cared for this patient.

Step 5 of 5 — Routing

Where is the patient coming from?

This determines which admission routing rules apply.

Routing

Is today a weekday or weekend / holiday?

Routing — ED weekday

What time is it now?

The 4 PM cutoff governs who handles the admission on a weekday.

Routing — ED weekday after 4 PM

Does this bounceback belong to the long call team?

After 4 PM, long call can admit their own bouncebacks only if they have not capped — 20 total patients, or the bounceback intern already has 10.

Routing — ED weekend

Does this bounceback belong to the long call team or a non-call team?

On weekends, only one GMed team is on long call and actively admitting. All other teams are non-call.

Routing — ED weekend, long call

What time is it now?

Long call can admit their own bouncebacks up to 6 PM on weekends.

Routing — ED weekend, non-call team

What time is it now?

Non-call teams can only accept bouncebacks before noon on weekends.

Routing — ICU weekday

Has the accepting team been notified by 4 PM?

The ICU must notify the accepting team by 4 PM on weekdays for the transfer to proceed today.

Routing — ICU weekend

Does this bounceback belong to the long call team or a non-call team?

Long call has no noon cutoff for ICU bouncebacks on weekends. Non-call teams must be notified by noon.

Routing — ICU weekend, non-call team

Has the accepting team been notified by noon?

Non-call teams on weekends must be notified by noon for the ICU transfer to proceed that day.
